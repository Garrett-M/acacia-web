Rush Acacia

Acacia is a unique fraternity. We were recolonized at UW in 2014 and have grown rapidly since. While we're nearing our charter, Acacia is still expanding quickly and that brings about a variety of opportunities that simply do not exist in other fraternities. Leadership opportunities present themselves from the first quarter of active membership, in addition to many opportunities for personal and academic growth over one's college career.

If you're interested in rushing Acacia or have any questions, contact our Rush Chairman, Jared Christenson, at (425) 736-9102. For a house tour, contact our House Manager, Spencer Kelly, at (360) 852-7427.

The Pledge Process

Pledging is first and foremost a process of familiarity. It is a time for a pledge to learn more about the fraternity and its members and for the members to learn more about the pledge. If, after the pledge process, an individual feels the fraternity is still a good fit and the members feel similarly, the pledge will then transition into active membership.

Acacia is a strictly non-hazing fraternity. Hazing is explicitly forbidden in the Laws of Acacia. We strive to create a positive environment for our men to grow: one of respect and dignity.
